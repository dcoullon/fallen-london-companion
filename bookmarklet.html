FL Companion — Bookmarklet

Shows echo values next to items gained or lost, plus upfront cost previews before you choose a branch. Works on any browser including Android Chrome — activate once per session after the game loads.

Desktop — drag to bookmarks bar

Drag this link onto your bookmarks bar. Then open Fallen London and click it once.

Android Chrome — step by step

  1. Bookmark any page (tap the star icon or ⋮ → Add to bookmarks).
  2. Open Bookmarks (⋮ → Bookmarks).
  3. Long-press the bookmark you just created → Edit bookmark.
  4. Delete the URL field completely and paste the code below. Keep the name, e.g. FL Companion.
  5. Save.
Every time you open Fallen London, tap that bookmark from the bookmarks list once the game has loaded. A brief "FL Companion active" banner confirms it's running for the session.

What it shows

  • After each action: echo value in green/red next to every item gained or lost, plus a net total when multiple items change.
  • Before choosing a branch: echo cost of required items shown below the icon (e.g. −0.50E under Vision of the Surface).
  • Rat Market Saturation: current boost tier and how far to the next threshold.
