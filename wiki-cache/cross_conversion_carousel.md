Source: https://fallenlondon.wiki/wiki/Making_Waves_(Guide)#Cross-Conversion_Carousel
Source: https://fallenlondon.wiki/wiki/Template:Cross_Conversion

Conversions marked ≈ produce 1-10 CP of Making Waves. All others (▸) produce nothing.
Carousel averages 1.62 CP Making Waves per action across all 13 steps.
